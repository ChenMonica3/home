Due Vite by Marco Mengoli

Artist Biography

On December 25, 1988, Marco Mengoni was born in Ronciglione. He began taking singing lessons when he was 14 years old and enrolled in a high school course for industrial design. Later, he began to perform with a five-piece vocal ensemble. He relocated to Rome after finishing high school and enrolled in a language programme there. He occasionally worked as a barman when he was in school and sang at weddings and piano bars.


Song and Performance Review

My first impression of this song was that it's a beautiful change from the high tempo pop songs that are usually present at Eurovision. During the performance, I'm honestly just watching the dancers in the background and how they seamlessly fall off the stars, only to be trampolined back up. The smoke that is gently moving around really adds to the mystical feel of the song. The singer has amazing vocals and I am pleased to see the audience waving around their phone flashlights in the background.
