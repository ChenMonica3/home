Due Vite by Marco Mengoli

Artist Biography

On December 25, 1988, Marco Mengoni was born in Ronciglione, where he also grew up. He began taking singing lessons when he was 14 years old and enrolled in a high school course for industrial design. Later, Mengoni began to perform with a five-piece vocal ensemble. He relocated to Rome after finishing high school and enrolled in a language programme there. He occasionally worked as a barman when he was in school and sang at weddings and piano bars.
