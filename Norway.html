Queen of Kings by Alessandra

Artist Biography

Alessandra was born on September 5, 2002, to an Italian father from Albenga and a Norwegian mother from Stathelle in Pietra Ligure, Savona. She was raised in Cisano sul Neva and she won the fifth season of VB Factor, a regional talent competition in the Val Bormida area, when she was twelve years old. She moved in with her maternal grandparents in Porsgrunn, Norway, after graduating from high school in 2021, then she relocated to Lillehammer to attend the "Lillehammer Institute of Music Production and Industries."


Song and Performance Review

The background vocals at the beginning of the song really give this an ethereal feel, like you're about to listen to the greatest folklore story of all time. This sort of mysterious feeling continues until the chorus, where you can see four backup dancers appearing from the shadows. The chorus is no doubt very catchy, which definitely adds to the popularity of the song and it's no wonder this song won Norway's National Finals, Melodi Grand Prix 2023. The performance by the backup dancers with the glowing sticks during the second chorus is also a nice touch that really blends in with the stage lighting. I think that the jury gave less points than I was expecting them to but I'm glad this song got as many televote points as it did.
