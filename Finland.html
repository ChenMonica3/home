Cha Cha Cha by Käärijä

Artist Biography

Käärijä was raised in the Ruskeasanta suburb of Vantaa, Greater Helsinki. While learning to play the drums, he realised his love for music, and he began making songs in 2014. His stage name, which was inspired by his love of gambling, is kääriä rahaa, which means "to make a quick buck".
