Artist Biography

Having Israeli parents, Noa Kirel was born in Ra'anana, Israel. Her father's family is Ashkenazi Jewish from Austria, while her mother's family is Moroccan-Jewish. Her father is the chief executive officer of Glassco Glass, an imported-glass company with its main office in the Barkan industrial park. Her mother runs an upscale clothing boutique.
