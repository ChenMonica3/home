Unicorn by Noa Kirel

Artist Biography

Noa Kirel was born in Ra'anana, Israel. Her father's family is Ashkenazi Jewish from Austria, while her mother's family is Moroccan-Jewish. Her father is the chief executive officer of Glassco Glass, an imported-glass company. Her mother runs an upscale clothing boutique.


Song and Performance Review

At the beginning of the song, I enjoy the effect that was created by the lights in the box. I also find it impressive how she can sing and perform a dance at the same time. The background dancers definitely add to the performance but I really liked when they left during the beginning dance break by the singer and then came back to perform my favourite dance moment of this years' show. However, I find the lyrics to be slightly rudimentary for a song contest. I was shocked when I saw this receive as many televote points as it did, with Israel being a controversial place at this moment.
